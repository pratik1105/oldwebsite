14 only, was I,  
but, you were 19.  
Realize,do you,  
Infinite memories,our,  
of (daily)horror movies,vulgar inside jokes(unfunny),  
that night turned into  
(unfunny) jokes inside vulgar movies.  
HORROR(daily) OF OUR MEMORIES:INFINITE.
You do realize ?  
19 were you  
but I was only 14.
